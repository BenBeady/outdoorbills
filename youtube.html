👉 How To Add Bill's Real Videos

1. Go to any video on youtube.com/@bill_leroy
2. Copy the video ID from the URL — the part after ?v= (example: dQw4w9WgXcQ)
3. Open youtube.html and find the video embeds
4. Replace the src in each <iframe> with: https://www.youtube.com/embed/YOUR_VIDEO_ID?rel=0&modestbranding=1
5. Update the title text below each embed too
